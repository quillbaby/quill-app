What Quill tracks

Everything new parents actually need.

Sleep & naps

Quick-tap timers, wake-window guidance based on your baby's age, nap trends, and gentle reminders when a nap window opens.

Feeding

Nursing timers (left/right), bottles with oz or ml, pumping sessions with simultaneous or sequential modes. Tracks every drop without making you do math.

Diapers

One tap to log wet, dirty, or both. Add color and consistency when it matters. See patterns over the week.

Milestones

First smile. First laugh. First steps. Capture moments with a photo and a note. Yours to keep — never uploaded anywhere.

Growth & food

Weight and height with WHO percentile context. Solid food introductions with allergen awareness. Vitamins, medications, and care events.

Built for multiple babies

Twins? Multiple children? Each baby has their own profile, log preferences, and notifications. Switch between them instantly.

Privacy, by design

Made for you, not for data.

Every other baby tracker collects your data. Many sell it. Quill takes a different approach: nothing leaves your device. Ever.

  • →
    No accounts. No sign-up, no email, no password to forget at 3am.
  • →
    No cloud. Every log, photo, and note stays on your phone. We have no server that holds your data.
  • →
    No tracking. No analytics. No ads. No third-party SDKs collecting behavior.
  • →
    Encrypted backups. When you back up, the file is encrypted with a password only you know. You choose where it lives — iCloud, Files, a thumb drive — Quill never sees it.
